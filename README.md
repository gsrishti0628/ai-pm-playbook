# AI PM Playbook
### Field notes from shipping agentic AI in production

This is a running record of what I'm learning as a PM building enterprise agentic AI products. No theory for its own sake. Everything here comes from real decisions, things that broke, and what we figured out after.

Written for PMs navigating the shift from AI-powered to AI-native product development.

---

## What's here

**[Agentic AI PM Principles](./agentic-ai-pm-principles.md)**
Three principles I developed shipping enterprise agentic AI: why autonomy has to be earned incrementally, why handoffs are harder than tasks, and why non-determinism changes what "done" means for a PM.

**[Multi-Agent Orchestration: Field Notes](./multi-agent-orchestration-field-notes.md)**
What actually goes wrong when you chain agents together in production — specifically, state continuity failures at handoffs, how to diagnose random failures vs logic errors, and how to set UAT thresholds when your system is non-deterministic. Includes the current state of how the industry is addressing this as of April 2026.

---

## How to use this

Read the principles file first if you want the mindset framing. Read the field notes if you're actively debugging or designing a multi-agent system and want the tactical stuff.

Both files are living documents. I update them as deployments progress and the field moves.
